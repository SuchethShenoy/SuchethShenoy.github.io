**Abstract**  
This patent describes the design of a robotic end effector for selective tea leaf harvesting. The proposed mechanism enables precise plucking of tea leaves while minimizing damage to the plant, addressing challenges associated with automation in unstructured agricultural environments. The design focuses on mechanical simplicity, adaptability, and compatibility with robotic manipulators used in agricultural applications.
